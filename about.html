The Story Behind Engineer Coffee

From Engineering Passion to the Perfect Cup

Engineer Coffee was born from a simple vision: what if we applied engineering principles to the world of coffee? Just like assembling a perfect machine, every cup of coffee requires precision, consistency, and attention to detail.

We believe that brewing the perfect coffee is like tuning a race engine – every parameter must be just right: water temperature, extraction time, grind size, and ratio. This is why our workshop is equipped with the best brewing equipment and baristas trained like engineers.

More than just a coffee shop, we are a community hub – for engineering students working on assignments, professionals needing fuel for meetings, and motorcycle enthusiasts who love to discuss things over coffee.
